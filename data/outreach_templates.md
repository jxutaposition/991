# Outreach Templates — Lele 2.0 Seed Raise (5/1–5/13)

Goal: 52 meetings in 13 days → 30+ booked → close $500K from VC + $500K from strategic angels.

---

## 1. Cold angel — LinkedIn connection request (300 char max)

> Hi [First], huge fan of your [specific thing — e.g., investment in X / essay on Y / role at Z]. I'm building [Lele 2.0 — one-line], backed by [warm name or notable signal]. Raising a seed; would love your read for 15 min if open. No pitch ask — just feedback.

Notes: lead with specific signal-of-attention, never generic. If they accept and don't reply, move to message #2 within 24 h.

---

## 2. Cold angel — follow-up DM after connection accepted

> Thanks for connecting, [First]. Quick context: [Lele 2.0 in 1 sentence — what + why-now]. Numbers so far: [traction line — users, revenue, growth rate, or specific milestone]. We're closing a seed in May with [lead/notable angel if any]. 15-min call this week to get your take? Tue/Wed/Thu best — Calendly: [link].

Notes: include 1 traction proof point. Calendly link makes scheduling trivial.

---

## 3. Warm angel — direct DM (already 1st-degree connection)

> [First] — long time. Quick ask: I'm raising a seed for [Lele 2.0, one line]. [One traction proof.] You're exactly the kind of operator I want around the table given your work on [specific thing]. 15 min next week? Calendly: [link]. Happy to send a memo first if useful.

Notes: warm = skip the "I'm a fan" preamble, get to the ask faster. Reference their specific expertise as the reason you're asking them.

---

## 4. Cold partner (post-5/6) — LinkedIn connection request

> Hi [First] — building [Lele 2.0, one line]. [Specific traction or notable backer.] Saw your investment in [adjacent portfolio co] — same thesis. Would value 20 min if you have a window in May.

Notes: ALWAYS reference a specific portfolio company in the partner's investment area. Generic "raising a round" requests get ignored at the partner level.

---

## 5. Partner — follow-up DM after connection accepted (or via warm intro email)

> Thanks for connecting, [First].
>
> [Lele 2.0]: [one-line product]. [One sentence on the why-now / market wedge.]
>
> Traction:
> • [metric 1]
> • [metric 2]
> • [metric 3]
>
> Round: [target raise size, lead status, target close date].
>
> Open to a 20–30 min call the week of [5/12 or 5/19]? Here's my Calendly: [link]. Memo attached if helpful: [Notion or Docsend link].
>
> Best,
> [Name]

Notes: 3 bullets max for traction. Round status (lead/no lead, close date) builds urgency. Always offer the memo.

---

## 6. Warm intro request (asking your network to forward you to a partner)

> Hey [Friend] — hope you're well. I noticed you're connected to [Partner Name] at [Firm]. Would you be willing to forward an intro? Forwardable below.
>
> ---
>
> [Friend], thanks for the intro.
>
> [Partner First] — [Lele 2.0 in one line]. [Why-now hook.] [Traction: 2 bullets.] Raising [size] seed targeting close [date].
>
> 20 min in May? Calendly: [link]. Memo: [link].
>
> Best, [Name]

Notes: Pre-write the forwardable so your friend's effort is just "press forward." This 3x's reply rate on warm intros.

---

## 7. After-meeting follow-up (within 4 hours)

> [First] — thanks for the time today. As discussed, sending:
> • Updated memo: [link]
> • [Specific thing they asked for — e.g., revenue cohort data, customer reference]
>
> Round close target: [date]. Happy to add you to the SAFE — minimum [$25K / $50K] for angels, [$250K] for firm checks. Let me know by [date] and I'll send docs.
>
> Best, [Name]

Notes: ALWAYS specify a date for next step. Ambiguity kills momentum on a fast close.

---

## Cadence rules

- Day 0: connection request
- Day +1 (after accept): DM #2
- Day +4 (no reply): one nudge — "circling back, here's the memo: [link]"
- Day +10 (no reply): drop. Move on.

## Daily targets (to hit 4 meetings/day = 52 over 13 days)

- Send 30 connection requests/day to maintain a 4-meeting/day booked rate (assumes ~30% accept, ~50% of those convert to a call).
- Front-load angels 4/30–5/5; partners 5/6 onward.

## Tracking

Log each outreach in `/data/outreach_log.csv` with columns: `date,name,bucket,channel,status,next_action_date,notes`.
