# HeyReach — Client Brief

*Last updated: 2026-03-11*

## What HeyReach is

LinkedIn outreach SaaS. Experts (agencies and power users) drive revenue via referral links, plan upgrades, and workspace access.

## The engagement

- Design and operate the Expert Program (tiering, scoring, incentives)
- Build automation workflows (Clay, Lovable, n8n, Notion)
- Advise on community-led growth strategy
