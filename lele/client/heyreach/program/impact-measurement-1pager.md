## North Star Metrics (prioritized)

Focus on 1–2 metrics where:
1. **Causation is known** — the input (our program) directly drives the outcome
2. **Strategic fit** — reflects where in the funnel HeyReach wants to grow the partner motion

**Top candidates:**
- **Referral revenue** — direct result of the expert/creator program; Tolt tracks per-expert attribution. High controllability. Recommended north star if funnel priority is conversion/retention.
- **# of partners onboarded + # of events held** — leading indicators (input metrics). What we focused on to date. Works well when the program is still in growth phase and letting sales handle enterprise pipeline downstream.

*Which to prioritize depends on a strategic call: upper funnel (impressions, reach, activation) vs. lower funnel (pipeline, revenue, retention). Recommend looping in RevOps or finance/data for attribution model design if going lower-funnel.*
