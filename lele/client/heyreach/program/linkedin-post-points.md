## What's confirmed and live

### Content Points (LinkedIn Posts)
- **1 reaction = 1 content point** — set by Lele, shipped Feb 24
- Source: Lele's Slack message (ts 1771900670): *"the LI mentions table (1 reaction = 1 content point, currently tracking posts from Feb)"*
- Implemented in: Clay LI mentions table → Lovable dashboard

### What's being tracked
- Reactions on LinkedIn posts mentioning HeyReach (via Clay social listening)
- Posts detected via Clay native LinkedIn signal, matched to expert by LinkedIn profile URL

### What's NOT yet agreed/implemented for LI posts
- Point values for **comments** (mentioned in Feb-17 meeting notes: "Reactors / Comments / # of posts" as signals, but no values set)
- Point values for **number of posts** (listed as a signal, not implemented)
- The Notion project plan (`30bc1c45-c9a0-80ba-a025-d2e15eb24415`) lists "LinkedIn Posts — Reactors / Comments / # of posts" without specific values for comments or posts
